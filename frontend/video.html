The Best Burger Recipe | How to Make Hamburger

Prep Time: 10 mins Cook Time: 10 mins Total Time: 20 mins Servings: 12 Yield: 12 sliders

Ingredients

  1. 1 (12 count) package Hawaiian rolls
  2. 3 tablespoons butter
  3. 1 clove garlic, minced
  4. 4 ounces thinly sliced deli ham
  5. 2 ounces salami
  6. 2 ounces pepperoni
  7. 6 to 8 leaves romaine lettuce
  8. 1 small tomato, sliced
  9. 4 slices red onion
  10. 1/4 cup banana peppers, plus 2 teaspoons brine
  11. 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  12. 1 1/2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  13. 1/2 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  14. 1/4 teaspoon salt
  15. 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  16. 1/4 cup mayonnaise
